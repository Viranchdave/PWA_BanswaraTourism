Privacy Policy for SAFAR (Spatial Application For Accessing Tourism Repository)

Effective Date: [25/05/2025]

Thank you for using the SAFAR. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Location Data (Optional)

We request access to your device's location to:

  • Display your current position on the map
  • Offer nearby places and navigation

Location data is used in real-time only. It is not stored, logged, or shared with any third party.

b. Weather Data

We use your approximate location (only if you enable it) to fetch real-time weather data for nearby attractions using the OpenWeatherMap API.

No personal data is sent to OpenWeatherMap—only location coordinates.

c. External Links

The app includes links to:

  • Google Maps for directions and location-based searches
  • Wikipedia and other websites for more information

These third-party sites may have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or data practices.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show your current location on the map
  • To offer personalized tourist recommendations
  • To fetch relevant weather data

3. Data Storage and Sharing

We do not store or share your personal data.

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

4. Your Privacy Choices

  • You may disable location permissions via your device settings.
  • You may uninstall the app to stop all information usage.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Security

We use standard mobile security practices. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted in the app or on our website if applicable.
