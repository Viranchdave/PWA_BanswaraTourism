Breathtaking Banswara

Located in the southern tip of Rajasthan State, Banswara District is unique in itself as it presents a kaleidoscopic view encompassing different shades of culture, nature, history, and so on that can give an unique opportunity for tourists to remember their journey to this wonderful place.

Owing to its exemplary geographic location on the upper catchment of Mahi River, Banswara District boasts plenty of riverine landforms such as waterfalls, rapids, river islands, caves, etc. The presence of river islands has made this District well known as the 'City of Hundred Islands' . In addition to these, Banswara District is home to the Bhil Tribal community. The rich cultural heritage of Bhils adds another important facet to this landscape that makes Banswara a breataking experience for any visitor to get immersed into the awe-inspiring blend and symbiosis of nature and culture. more

Best Time to Visit Banswara

June to September: Owing to good amount of rainfall, Banswara seems paradise during the Monsoon months

Getting There

Nearest Bus Station: Banswara Bus Stand
